Hay's Clay Co. is a small batch clay earring business.

About this project

My client Hailey needed a website to display her clay earrings for sale and software to help her handle the ecommerce. I recommended she use Shopify to handle the ecommerce and I used their website builder to build her website.

I used one of the basic shopify themes as a template and then edited some code to make small changes to the design as she requested. I kept the website simple with the main focus being on the product. To build trust with potential customers I included her instagram feed on the landing page.
